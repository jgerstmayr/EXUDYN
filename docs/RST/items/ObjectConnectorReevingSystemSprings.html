ObjectConnectorReevingSystemSpringsÔÉÅ

A rD reeving system defined by a list of torque-free and friction-free sheaves or points that are connected with one rope (modelled as massless spring). The force is assumed to be constant all over the rope. The sheaves or connection points are defined by \(nr\) rigid body markers \([m_0, \, m_1, \, \ldots, \, m_nr-1]\). At both ends of the rope there may be a prescribed motion coupled to a coordinate marker each, given by \(m_c0\) and \(m_c1\) .
